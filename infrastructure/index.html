GHSSN

G H S S NEELESWARAM was established in 1924 and it is managed by the Department of Education. It is located in Rural area. It is located in KUNNAMANGALAM block of KOZHIKODE district of Kerala. The school consists of Grades from 1 to 12. The school is Co-educational and it doesn't have an attached pre-primary section. The school is Not Applicable in nature and is not using school building as a shift-school. Malayalam is the medium of instructions in this school. This school is approachable by all weather road. In this school academic session starts in April.

We ensure all facilities for your kids to build thier knowledge

Infrastructure

The school has Government building. It has got 25 classrooms for instructional purposes. All the classrooms are in good condition. It has 2 other rooms for non-teaching activities. The school has Pucca boundary wall. The school has a playground. The school has a library and has 7165 books in its library. The school has 47 computers for teaching and learning purposes and all are functional.
